How to Export the Schedule CSV

  1. 01Open bookmarklet/install.html in your browser and drag the bookmarklet to your bookmarks bar.
  2. 02Go to Banner Course Schedule and log in.
  3. 03Select the current term and wait for the page to fully load.
  4. 04Click the bookmarklet — it will loop through all subjects and download a combined CSV automatically.
  5. 05Upload that CSV file here. The schedule updates instantly — no server restart needed.
